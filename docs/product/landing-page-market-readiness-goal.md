# WolfWhale Landing Page Market Readiness Goal

Status: active workstream under the broader WolfWhale school platform launch goal.
Public target: https://wolfwhale.ca

## Objective

Make the public WolfWhale landing page credible for school administrators, teachers, families, investors, and event contacts. The page should quickly explain what WolfWhale does, why it is different, and why a school should book a pilot conversation.

## Current Creative Rules

- Keep the Monstera water background direction.
- Keep the WolfWhale logo large and visible.
- Render the WolfWhale wordmark in Times New Roman with normal tight spacing.
- Do not use the standalone acronym "LMS" in visible public landing copy.
- Use full phrases when needed: learning management, student information, school platform, family visibility.
- Keep copy short, concrete, and school-buyer focused.
- Keep fish rewards as a supporting differentiator, not the whole pitch.

## Research Signals

- Canvas leads with scale, accessibility, open integrations, and proven usage volume.
- Brightspace leads with one platform, AI assistance, insights, course authoring, and services.
- Edsby leads with K-12 focus, parent connection, assessment/reporting, and one system for all grades.
- Moodle leads with educator ownership, flexibility, security, scalability, accessibility, and control.
- SaaS landing best practice: the first screen should answer what it does, who it is for, why it matters, and drive one clear action.

## WolfWhale Positioning

WolfWhale is a Canadian school platform that connects learning management, student information, family visibility, and playful student motivation in one friendly product.

Primary buyer promise:

> A school can pilot WolfWhale with real courses, grades, attendance, messages, files, family views, and privacy controls without making the experience feel corporate or overwhelming.

## Landing Page Quality Gates

- First viewport shows the product name, logo, short offer, and primary action without scrolling.
- Copy is benefit-led, not a feature dump.
- Schools can immediately see classroom, records, family, privacy, and pilot value.
- Every claim is defensible from current product capability or marked as a pilot path.
- Desktop, mobile, and small mobile have no horizontal overflow or clipped text.
- The demo and contact paths work.
- The page deploys cleanly to both `wolfwhale.ca` and `www.wolfwhale.ca`.

## Continuous Loop

1. Audit the live page for clarity, trust, conversion path, visual quality, responsive fit, and broken claims.
2. Research competitor positioning and school/SaaS landing patterns.
3. Improve one focused slice: hero, section copy, proof, CTA, role story, trust story, or contact path.
4. Run lint, typecheck, production build, and responsive Playwright QA.
5. Commit, push, deploy, and live-smoke both custom domains.
6. Record any remaining landing blockers before the next loop.
