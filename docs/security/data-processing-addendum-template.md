# Data Processing Addendum Template

Date: 2026-05-10

Status: placeholder for counsel review. This is not legal advice and is not ready to sign.

## Parties

- Customer: the school, district, university, or board contracting for WolfWhale LMS.
- Processor/service provider: WolfWhale Inc.
- Data subjects: students, parents/guardians, teachers, administrators, and support users.

## Processing Purpose

WolfWhale processes school data only to provide LMS services: authentication, courses, sections, assignments, submissions, grading, attendance, messaging, reporting, file storage, companion rewards, support, security, backups, and audit logging.

## Data Categories

- Student profile and enrollment data.
- Parent/guardian link data.
- Assignment submissions, private uploaded files, grades, rubric feedback, attendance, messages, calendar events, and reports.
- Teacher/admin account data, course resources, operational audit logs, and support metadata.

## Processor Commitments

- Process school data only on documented school instructions and product support requirements.
- Do not sell student data.
- Do not use student data for advertising or cross-context behavioral profiling.
- Maintain role-based access controls, RLS, private storage, audit logs, encryption in transit, and encryption at rest where supported by infrastructure.
- Require subprocessors to protect school data under written terms.
- Notify the customer of a confirmed security incident according to the breach runbook and contract.
- Support export, correction, deletion, and retention workflows according to school instructions and legal obligations.

## Customer Commitments

- Provide lawful instructions and required notices/consents.
- Maintain accurate school roster, role, section, and guardian-link data.
- Notify WolfWhale promptly of deactivated users, incorrect guardian links, access concerns, or suspected incidents.

- Retention schedule.
- Incident notification contacts.
- Product-specific RLS/security validation evidence.
